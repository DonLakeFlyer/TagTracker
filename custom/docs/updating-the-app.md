# Updating the Tag Tracker App

Here is how to update to a new app version.

## Copy the new app to the controller SD Card

1. Power off the controller.
1. The SD Card slot is on the bottom right portion of the area covered by the rubber cover.
1. Press the SD Card to eject it.
1. Put the SD Card into an SD Card reader on your computer.
1. Copy the `QGroundControl.apk` file sent to you to the root of the SD Card.
1. Eject the SD Card from your computer.
1. Put it back in the controller SD Card slot. Note that it goes in upside-down.

## Uninstall the old version

1. Power on the controller. Click power once. Click and hold again until the logo appears. Once it starts up, click the **Home** button.
1. Swipe down from the top of the screen. Touch the **Settings** gear icon.
1. Scroll down until you see the **Apps** entry. Select it.
1. Scroll down until you see the **Tag Tracker** entry. Select it.
1. Select **UNINSTALL**.

## Install the new version

1. Swipe down from the top again.
1. Select **Samsung SD card**.
1. Select the `QGroundCo...` entry. Not the one that starts with `._`.
1. Select **INSTALL**.
1. When the install completes, select **OPEN**.
1. Select **Allow/Ok** on any pop-ups.
1. The new version of **Tag Tracker** should be ready to go.
